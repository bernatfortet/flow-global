Flow is a new social club and teaching center being built to strengthen and expand the acumen, power, and thriving of women, globally. In sumptuous physical space, we provide a gathering point for inspired connections. With tools and services, we support our members’ financial literacy, total wellbeing and community impact.

Our offerings will range from service-oriented and technological conveniences to capacity building educational experiences.

Membership is available to self-proclaimed women, age 18+. We support the flourishing of community through a commitment to serving women's needs. Visitors and guests of any gender are warmly welcomed on specific days, community events, and tailored programs.

As a global club with a long-term vision and strong social and ethical commitments, we prioritize access and impact through a robust and anonymously sponsored membership fund. In the next decade we envision Flow chapters spanning six continents linking millions of women to each other, to local and to global causes.

More

In addition to providing beautiful space for our members, we work with programming partners who serve women, including community leaders, creative mavens, scholars, private coaches, career and financial experts, and spiritual luminaries. Offering a spectrum of services through our programming partners opens the door to access, knowledge, and options for the Flow community. In partnerships, we select for experience, responsibility, talent, and authenticity.

Flow will also offer discovery tools and foundational programs for our members who wish to focus and strengthen their capabilities and skills. We collaborate with each member as she nourishes and unleashes her own abilities through our workshops, sisterhoods, and mentoring.

More

Flow Global, Inc. is created with principle and purpose at its core. Our organizational structure is a hybrid non-profit/for-profit enterprise.

Demonstrating societal contribution and impact, inclusive and aligned with personal thriving, is the highest aspiration of our organization.

We uphold a stewardship model over an ownership model and actively work toward healthy financial self-sufficiency over dependence and debt.

Flow Global, Inc. does not exist in order to make individuals wealthy at the expense of its goals, mission or members.

We value:

  • Thoughtful, heartfelt action.
  • Diversity of ideas and people.
  • Clear lines of responsibility.
  • A joyous, creative and soulful work environment.
